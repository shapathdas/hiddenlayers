AI research is advancing at an incredible pace, and understanding its current state has never been more important.

That’s why we are creating The Frontier List — an initiative to index, evaluate, and showcase the most impactful AI research from the past year. The Frontier List aims to provide a clear snapshot of the global AI research landscape.

What are we looking for?

We’re seeking papers that introduce groundbreaking ideas, challenge existing paradigms, and drive the next wave of AI innovation — research with the potential to unleash widely impactful industrial applications across sectors.

Why nominate?

We don’t want to miss any research you believe is truly groundbreaking — work that pushes the boundaries of what’s possible in AI.

If your nomination is featured in the final Frontier List, you’ll have the opportunity to present your unique perspective to a diverse, high-impact audience.
