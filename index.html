We are developing the Frontier List.

A close collaboration between industry and academia aimed at identifying and producing a list of the most distinguished AI research papers published worldwide each year.

The Frontier List is designed to spotlight work that meaningfully advances the state of the art, sets new research directions, and has the potential to reshape both scientific understanding and real-world applications. By combining academic rigor with industry insight, the list stands out as a trusted, cross-sector benchmark for truly frontier-level AI innovation.
